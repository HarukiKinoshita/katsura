Notice on unauthorized access to Peatix



Peatix, which is a ticket service we used at TEDxTsukuba in the past, attacked by a third party from October 16th to 17th, 2020. It was reported that up to 6.77 million personal information such as “name”, “email address”, and ” password” have been extracted. (It seems that the fact that payment-related information such as credit card information and financial institution account information, event participation history, data acquired by the questionnaire form function for participants, address, telephone company, etc. was extracted has not been confirmed. It is said that other matters are currently under investigation. Please see the following URL for details.
